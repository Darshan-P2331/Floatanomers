Ecommerce Masterclass,India Internet Day 2020 & More
  Admin
  January 14, 2019
2 Commets

TiE Mumbai will be hosting an ecommerce masterclass With Flintstop’s founder Tejas Jhaveri. Mumbai-based ecommerce platform Flintstop is an online portal that deals in quirky, innovative and unique products. Jhaveri will be speaking about his journey as an entrepreneur and sharing his lessons with the other budding entrepreneurs in this segment.

Internet and Mobile Association of India (IAMAI) is hosting a Virtual Fintech Festival (VFF) to bring the brightest minds in the financial and fintech world together in an innovative digital format. The event aims to showcase India’s innovation in the fintech space to the global audience and also attract viewership for international solutions. At the festival, there will be opportunities to meet, learn, to make deals and enter into partnerships on a completely virtual platform. The two-day festival will organise more than 40 sessions hosting more than 150 Indian and international speakers. The industry body is expecting 3,500 attendees across 20 countries. Startup Summit Live is being held on July 24, 2020 in order to give a common platform to over 10K entrepreneurs, investors, corporate representatives, government officials and influencers. The speaker list for the event includes Tesla and SpaceX investor Tim Draper, investor Sir Martin Sorrel, angel investor Fabrice Grinda, Innov8’s Ritesh Malik, Shopclues and Droom’s Sandeep Aggarwal, LetsVenture’s Shanti Mohan and more.
